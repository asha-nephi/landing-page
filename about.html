Our Story

Bridging the Gap for Diaspora Owners.

Deseret Facility Management LTD was founded on a single premise: distance should never compromise property integrity or owner peace of mind.

Born from Personal Challenge

Our founders experienced first-hand the difficulties of managing premium Lagos properties while residing abroad. The lack of structural accountability and "opaque" service charge budgets led to a vision for a professional alternative.

Today, Deseret stands as a beacon of professionalism in the Nigerian facility management space, combining local expertise with international standards of transparency.

Our Mission

To preserve structural assets and enhance quality of life through preventive management and ethical transparency.

Our Vision

To be the primary trusted partner for African property owners globally, redefining trust in facility stewardship.

Our Values

Proactive Prevention, Absolute Integrity, Data-Driven Results, and Professional Empathy.
