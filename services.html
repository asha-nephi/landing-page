Our Services

We provide a comprehensive suite of facility management solutions specifically engineered for the high standards of Lagos' luxury properties and the needs of diaspora owners.

01. Structural Maintenance

Proactive Structural Integrity Monitoring

Lagos' coastal climate can be harsh on structural elements. We conduct bi-annual integrity audits to detect moisture ingress, concrete fatigue, and structural shifts before they become costly repairs.

  • Quarterly MEP Audits
  • Preventive Roofing Inspections
  • Plumbing & Electrical Safety Checks
02. Security & Access

Advanced Security Solutions

Security is not just about guards; it's about systems. We integrate physical presence with digital oversight, ensuring your property is monitored 24/7 with verifiable access logs.

  • Professional Staffed Security
  • CCTV Remote Monitoring Access
  • Biometric Access Controls
03. Financial Transparency

Complete Service Charge Accountability

We eliminate the "hidden costs" often associated with property management in Nigeria. Our clients receive itemized, real-time expenditure reports via our digital platform.

  • Real-time Budgeting Apps
  • Verified Vendor Invoicing
  • Transparent Sinking Fund Management
